Standing Water in Your Crawlspace — Middle Tennessee

Standing water indicates drainage problems. Here are quick signs and safe first steps.

3 Quick Signs of Drainage or Water Intrusion

  • Visible puddles, saturated soil, or water marks on foundation walls.
  • Sump pumps present but not operating, or frequent backups.
  • Rust or corrosion on metal components near the floor level.

What To Do Next

Identify water entry points (downspouts, grading, plumbing) and avoid electrical hazards. Often the immediate fixes are improved drainage, sump installation, or redirecting downspouts.
