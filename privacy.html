Privacy Policy

We keep things simple and respect your privacy. This page explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

If you request the checklist via our form, we collect the details you provide (like name, email, photos, and any notes). We use Plausible for privacy-friendly analytics (no cookies, no cross-site tracking).

How we use your information

We use your info to send the checklist, provide guidance, and—only if you ask—introduce you to vetted local pros. We don’t sell your data.

Data retention

We retain submitted details only as long as needed to provide guidance or fulfill your request.

Your choices

You can request deletion or updates anytime: hello@crawlspacehelptn.xyz.

Third parties

If you ask for introductions, we may share your contact details and basic project context with a vetted local professional. We’ll only share what’s needed to coordinate an introduction.
