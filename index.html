Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need full crawlspace encapsulation?

Not always. Sometimes simple fixes — better grading/gutters, sealing vents seasonally, or adding a dehumidifier — are enough. Encapsulation makes sense when humidity stays high, water enters after rains, or you see mold/wood damage.

What’s included in a proper encapsulation?

Continuous vapor barrier (taped and sealed), sealed walls/rim-joist (for sealed crawls), drainage and a sump where needed, and controlled humidity (dehumidifier or HVAC supply per code).

How much does it cost in Middle Tennessee?

Typical full encapsulations run about $6–$15k depending on square footage, barrier thickness, drainage, dehumidifier, and access.
