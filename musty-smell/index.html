3 Quick Signs the Smell Is From Your Crawlspace

  • Persistent musty odor when windows are closed and HVAC runs.
  • Visible mold or staining on floor joists, insulation, or vapor barrier.
  • Higher humidity readings in lower-floor rooms compared to outdoors.

What To Do Next

Check for standing water, sagging or missing vapor barrier, and visible mold. Take a few photos and start the 2-minute form so we can recommend the right scope—often drainage and a continuous vapor barrier are the immediate fixes.
