Mold in a crawlspace can threaten indoor air quality and structure—spot it early with these signs.

3 Quick Signs of Crawlspace Mold

  • Black or discolored patches on wood or insulation.
  • Musty smell that worsens in damp weather.
  • Allergy-like symptoms that improve when away from home.

What To Do Next

Document the area with photos, avoid disturbing mold (which can spread spores), and get an assessment—often moisture control and proper barrier installation are the cure.
